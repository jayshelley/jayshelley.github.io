About Jay

Jay Shelley is a Utah resident passionate about education and innovation. With a focus on student success and community engagement, Jay is running to ensure Timpanogos thrives post-Alpine split. Show More

Jay brings a rich background in technology, having worked for over a decade in software development, which fuels his vision for expanding STEM programs in Timpanogos schools. As a parent of two Timpanogos students, he understands the daily challenges families face and is committed to bridging the gap between home and school.

Jay’s experience extends to community leadership, where he has volunteered with local education initiatives. Show More

He has served on the PTA for five years, advocating for better resources and teacher support. This hands-on role has given him insight into the needs of both educators and students, shaping his commitment to a collaborative district.

My Platform
