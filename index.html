About Jay

I have a Masters of Accounting from BYU and studied entrepreneurship. My Platform

Protect Teacher Salaries aims to ensure competitive pay to retain educators during the district transition. Show More

This initiative includes negotiating with local government for sustainable funding and creating a retention bonus program to address the post-split staffing challenges.

Ensure Equitable Funding seeks fair resources for all Timpanogos schools post-split. Show More

This involves analyzing current disparities and proposing a weighted funding model to prioritize under-resourced schools, ensuring every student has equal opportunity.

Expand STEM Programs prepares students for tech careers with enhanced education. Show More

This plan includes partnering with tech companies for internships and updating curricula with cutting-edge tools, aiming to build a future-ready workforce in Timpanogos.
