About Jay

Jay Shelley is a Utah resident passionate about education and innovation. With a focus on student success and community engagement, Jay is running to ensure Timpanogos thrives post-Alpine split. Show More

Jay brings a rich background in technology, having worked for over a decade in software development, which fuels his vision for expanding STEM programs in Timpanogos schools. As a parent of two Timpanogos students, he understands the daily challenges families face and is committed to bridging the gap between home and school.

His campaign is driven by a belief that education should empower every child, not just cater to external mandates. Jay plans to leverage his experience to foster collaboration with teachers and parents, ensuring the district remains responsive and innovative for decades to come.

My Platform

  • Protect Teacher Salaries: Competitive pay to retain educators during the district transition.
  • Ensure Equitable Funding: Fair resources for all Timpanogos schools post-split.
  • Expand STEM Programs: Prepare students for tech careers with enhanced education.
